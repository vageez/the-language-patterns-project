## Core research question

> What recurring structures make language interpretable, and how can they be described precisely without mistaking hypotheses for established knowledge?

The project is still establishing its research architecture. The active hypotheses shown in the [current state](./current-state/) are working propositions, not accepted conclusions.

## Current research

Discovery 001 is establishing the website, metadata, artifact, and traceability architecture that future research will use. No canonical observations, experiments, or discoveries have been published yet.
