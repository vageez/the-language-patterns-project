---
title: Corpus
description: The categorized and traceable evidence base used by the research program.
---

The Corpus contains examples used to motivate, test, challenge, and reproduce research claims. Every entry preserves its source, context, category, excerpt or structured representation, and usage rights.

Initial categories include conversation, literature, programming, science, law, philosophy, children's books, journalism, religion, and poetry.

Hypotheses and experiments should reference canonical corpus entries. No corpus entries have been published yet. Start one with `templates/corpus-entry-template.md`.
