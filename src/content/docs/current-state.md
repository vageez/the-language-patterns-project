## Accepted decisions

- The project is a living, versioned specification.
- Discoveries and specifications are separate artifacts.
- Research begins with discovery rather than definition.
- Historical reasoning is preserved.
- Evidence takes precedence over intuition.
- Multiple competing hypotheses may coexist.
- Research produces Specification; Specification does not produce Research.
- The evidence chain must be navigable in both directions.

## Active hypotheses

These are active research propositions, not accepted conclusions.

- **H-0001:** Language is the expression of relationships.
- **H-0002:** Relationships are more fundamental than language.
- **H-0003:** Meaning emerges through interpretation rather than existing inside words.
- **H-0004:** Language constrains an interpretation space rather than transmitting fixed meaning.
- **H-0005:** Human communication maintains an implicit Active Scope analogous to computational scope.
