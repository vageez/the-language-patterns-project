Labels always appear as text and never depend on color alone.

## Status

`draft`, `active`, `accepted`, `superseded`, `historical`, and `deprecated` describe a document’s lifecycle.

## Maturity

`observation`, `hypothesis`, `candidate`, `validated`, `core`, and `deprecated` describe how far a concept has progressed through investigation.

## Confidence

`low`, `medium`, `high`, and `not_applicable` communicate the strength of present support. Confidence does not replace evidence or validation records.
