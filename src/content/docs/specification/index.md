The Specification contains the project's stable accepted knowledge. Every substantive claim here must be traceable to research evidence.

Nothing enters the Specification merely because it is elegant or persuasive. Accepted discoveries produce specification changes through review.

## Structure

- [Foundations](./foundations/) define the philosophical and structural basis of the project.
- [Ontology](./ontology/) defines what exists in the framework.
- [Primitive nodes](./primitive-nodes/), [relationships](./primitive-relationships/), and [modifiers](./primitive-modifiers/) formalize the minimal elements.
- [Language patterns](../patterns/) catalog accepted recurring structures.

The Specification is expected to change more slowly than Research, but it remains versioned and revisable when new evidence requires it.
