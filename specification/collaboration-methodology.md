# Collaboration Methodology

The application should present the project's collaboration methodology as a first-class specification.

Visitors should understand not only **what** the project is researching, but **how** the research is conducted.

The Collaboration Methodology page should explain:

- Why the project uses human–AI collaboration.
- The Philosopher research role.
- The Systems Engineer research role.
- That research roles describe cognitive responsibilities rather than individuals.
- That ideas are evaluated independently of their origin.
- That evidence, coherence, explanatory power, and refinement determine whether a proposal becomes canonical.

The Collaboration Methodology should appear alongside the project's Vision, Manifesto, Constitution, and Methodology, reinforcing that the research process is considered part of the project's architecture.
